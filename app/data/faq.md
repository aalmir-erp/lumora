# Urban Services FAQ

## Booking & Cancellation
- **Lead time:** Same-day bookings if requested before 12:00. Otherwise next-day.
- **Cancellation:** Free up to 4 hours before the slot. Within 4 hours, 50% charge. No-shows charged in full.
- **Reschedule:** Free up to 2 hours before the slot.

## Payment
- Cash, card on arrival, or online payment link sent on confirmation.
- Corporate clients: monthly invoicing on request.
- 5% VAT included on all displayed prices unless stated otherwise.

## Staff & Safety
- All cleaners are background-checked, uniformed, and insured under our liability policy.
- Female-only crews available on request (Sat–Thu).
- We carry our own equipment and eco-friendly supplies. If you prefer your own products, just let us know.

## Quality
- Not satisfied? We'll re-clean the affected area free of charge within 24 hours.
- 5-star rated team — we ask for honest feedback after every job.

## Areas
- Dubai (all areas), Sharjah, Ajman, parts of Umm Al Quwain.
- Abu Dhabi: yes, with a 50 AED travel surcharge.

## Pet & Allergy
- We can dispatch hypoallergenic-supplies crews on request.
- Inform us about pets at booking — we'll send a pet-friendly team.

## Data & Privacy
- We never share your address, phone, or payment details with third parties.
