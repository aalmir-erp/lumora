Lumora Service Partner Agreement

Version 1.0 · Effective from your acceptance during partner registration · Last updated 1 May 2026

This Service Partner Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between Lumora Home Services LLC, a UAE company ("Lumora"), and the natural or juridical person whose details are submitted during partner registration (the "Partner", "you"). It governs the supply of service-delivery capacity by the Partner under the Lumora brand and Lumora's quality, pricing and operating standards.

1. Relationship

The Partner operates as an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employer-employee, agency, partnership, joint venture or franchise relationship. Each party bears its own taxes, social contributions, payroll, end-of-service gratuity, work permits, and visa costs in respect of its own personnel.

From the customer's perspective, however, the Service is delivered under the Lumora brand: the Partner accepts that the customer-facing brand, pricing, terms, complaint resolution and refund decisions sit with Lumora, and the Partner agrees not to identify itself, its company name, its own logo, its own pricing or its own contact details to any Lumora customer.

2. Scope of services

The Partner agrees to perform the categories of services activated in its partner profile (e.g. deep cleaning, AC, pest control), in the geographic area(s) accepted, at the rates configured in Lumora's system, and in accordance with Lumora's published Service-Level Standards and Standard Operating Procedures (the "SOP") as amended from time to time.

3. Brand & non-circumvention

3.1 Brand standards

The Partner and any of its personnel deployed to a Lumora job must:

  • wear Lumora-approved uniform and ID badge;
  • introduce themselves as part of the Lumora team;
  • use only Lumora-approved tools, supplies and PPE that pass inspection;
  • follow Lumora's pre-arrival, on-site and post-service scripts and SOPs;
  • not display, distribute or mention any business card, leaflet, branded vehicle decal, social-media handle, banking detail or alternative phone number belonging to the Partner;
  • not photograph the customer's premises beyond pre/post-job evidence in the Lumora app, and never share such photos externally;
  • not solicit tips, donations, reviews on private platforms, or extra cash payments.

3.2 Non-circumvention

The Partner agrees, for the duration of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months thereafter, not to:

  • solicit, contact, accept work from or transact with any customer first introduced through Lumora outside the Lumora platform;
  • refer Lumora customers to a competing service or to its own direct channel;
  • retain, copy or process customer contact details for any purpose other than fulfilling the specific job assigned by Lumora;
  • induce any Lumora staff or other Partner to leave Lumora.

Breach of this clause entitles Lumora to (i) immediate termination, (ii) liquidated damages equal to AED 5,000 per affected customer, and (iii) any further loss Lumora can prove, without prejudice to injunctive relief.

4. Pricing & commissions

4.1 Customer price

Lumora sets and displays the customer-facing price. The Partner cannot quote, modify or invoice the customer directly.

4.2 Partner payout

Unless an alternative split is agreed in writing in the Partner's profile, Lumora retains a platform fee equal to 20% of the gross customer price on each completed booking. The remaining 80% is paid to the Partner via weekly bank transfer, net of any chargebacks, refunds, damage settlements, customer credits and VAT adjustments attributable to the Partner's performance.

4.3 VAT

Both parties are responsible for their own VAT registrations and filings. Lumora invoices the customer in its own name and is the merchant of record. Partner payout statements separately identify the VAT-adjusted amount.

4.4 Cancellations

If a customer cancels less than 2 hours before the slot or no-shows, Lumora retains the cancellation fee in full to cover overhead. Partner is not paid for incomplete jobs unless Lumora's dispatch error caused the failure.

5. Quality, ratings & suspension

Partners are continuously rated on punctuality, quality, customer rating, complaint rate and SOP compliance. A Partner whose rolling 30-day metrics fall below the thresholds in the SOP may be (i) reduced in priority, (ii) suspended pending re-training, or (iii) terminated. The thresholds and consequences are published in the Partner Portal and may be updated with 14 days' notice.

6. Insurance & liability

6.1 Partner insurance

The Partner must maintain, at its own cost, a valid UAE third-party liability insurance with cover of not less than AED 250,000 per incident and workers'-compensation cover for all personnel deployed to Lumora jobs. Certificates must be uploaded annually.

6.2 Indemnity

The Partner indemnifies and holds Lumora harmless against any claim, fine, loss or expense (including reasonable legal fees) arising from (a) acts or omissions of its personnel; (b) breach of this Agreement; (c) breach of UAE labour, immigration, tax or commercial law; (d) damage to customer property caused by the Partner; (e) injury to any person caused by the Partner.

6.3 Set-off

Lumora may set off any amount owed to it by the Partner under this clause against any payout otherwise due to the Partner.

7. Confidentiality & data protection

The Partner will treat all customer personal data disclosed to it as confidential, use it only to perform the specific assigned job, store it on Lumora-issued devices/apps only, never copy it to personal devices or third-party tools, and delete it from any temporary location within 24 hours of job completion. The Partner acknowledges that Lumora is the data controller of customer data and the Partner is a data processor acting solely on Lumora's documented instructions, in accordance with UAE Federal Decree Law No. 45 of 2021.

8. Compliance

The Partner warrants that it holds all licences, registrations, work permits, visas and approvals required to perform the contracted services in the UAE, that its personnel are legally entitled to work in the UAE, and that it will provide proof on Lumora's request.

9. Term & termination

This Agreement begins on acceptance and continues until terminated. Either party may terminate for convenience on 15 days' written notice. Lumora may terminate immediately for: breach of brand or non-circumvention clauses; serious customer complaints; insurance lapse; unlawful conduct; or material breach uncured for 7 days. Termination does not affect already-completed bookings or accrued indemnities, confidentiality and non-circumvention obligations, which survive for 12 months.

10. Intellectual property

Lumora's name, logo, mascot ("Lumi"), uniforms, app, training materials and SOPs are Lumora's exclusive property. Partner is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence to use them solely while delivering Lumora bookings.

11. Force majeure

Neither party is liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control: government action, pandemic, war, fire, flood, network outage, supply-chain failure or extreme weather. The affected party must notify the other promptly and use reasonable efforts to mitigate.

12. Governing law & disputes

UAE law applies. Any dispute will first be discussed in good faith between authorised signatories for 14 days; failing resolution, the dispute is submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Dubai.

13. Entire agreement

This Agreement, together with the SOP, the partner-portal pricing schedule and any signed addenda, constitutes the entire agreement and supersedes all prior arrangements. Amendments are valid only if (a) made through the Partner Portal with confirmation in writing or (b) signed by both parties.

14. Acceptance

By clicking "I accept" during partner registration, by uploading documentation, or by accepting the first booking, the Partner agrees to be bound by this Agreement on behalf of itself and any of its personnel deployed to Lumora jobs.
