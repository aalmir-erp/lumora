How to compare UAE home-services providers

A brand-free buyer's guide. We don't list third-party brand names or their pricing here, because specific claims about other companies can become inaccurate within weeks. Instead, here's how to think about the four provider archetypes in the UAE market — and where Servia sits.

Archetype A

App-based booking platforms

You install an app, browse a catalogue, pay by card. Polished UX, predictable hourly rates, but coverage is usually limited to Dubai or Dubai plus Abu Dhabi.

📌 The 8-point check before booking from anyone

  • Emirate coverage — does it cover where you actually live?
  • Service breadth — can the same provider handle the next job too?
  • Damage and theft cover — what's the AED limit and who pays the claim?
  • Time from booking to arrival — same-day, next-day, or scheduled-only?
  • Pricing transparency — is the quote final or are add-ons charged on the day?
  • Payment options — card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, WhatsApp, cash on delivery?
  • Redo or refund policy — how many days, what does it cover, how to claim?
  • Background-check policy — are technicians vetted and how is it proven?
