🗣 Voice trigger setup

Three ways to fire your shortcut by voice. Each uses the same trigger URL.

🅰️ Google Assistant Routine (phone)
  1. Open the Google Home app on your phone
  2. Tap the Routines tab → + Add a routine
  3. Add Starter → "When I say something" → type your trigger phrase (e.g. "Servia tow")
  4. Add Action → Open URL → paste your shortcut URL
  5. Save. Now say "Hey Google, Servia tow" anywhere.
🍎 Apple Shortcut (iPhone / Siri)
  1. Open the Shortcuts app
  2. Tap + → Add Action → search "Open URL" → paste your URL
  3. Tap the title at top → name it (e.g. "Servia tow")
  4. Done. Say "Hey Siri, Servia tow" anywhere.
🟧 IFTTT (Nest speakers, even without phone)
  1. Sign up free at ifttt.com
  2. + New Applet → If "Google Assistant — Say a simple phrase" → type your trigger
  3. Then "Webhooks — Make a web request" → paste your shortcut URL
  4. Method: GET · Save. Now any Nest speaker can fire it.

Heads-up: if your shortcut requires PIN, voice triggers will fail unless you hit the URL on the same phone you've signed in to Servia on. We auto-prompt for PIN there.
