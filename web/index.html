Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Lumora.

How much does deep cleaning a 2-bedroom apartment cost in Dubai?

Deep cleaning a 2-bedroom apartment with Lumora starts from 400 AED including 5% VAT. The exact price depends on size and any add-ons. Get an instant quote in chat — Lumi computes it in 5 seconds.

Do you cover Sharjah, Ajman and Abu Dhabi?

Yes — Lumora covers Dubai (all areas), Sharjah, Ajman, parts of Umm Al Quwain, and Abu Dhabi with a small 50 AED travel surcharge.

Are your cleaners background-checked and insured?

Every Lumora vendor is background-checked, uniformed, and covered under our liability insurance. Female-only crews on request (Sat–Thu).

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes. Cancel is free up to 4 hours before; within 4 hours, 50% charge. Reschedule is free up to 2 hours before.

What languages does Lumora support?

English, Arabic (العربية), Hindi (हिन्दी), and Filipino. Switch language top-right or just type in your language.

How quickly can I get a cleaner?

Same-day if you book before 12:00 — typically dispatched within 2 hours. Otherwise next-day slots are nearly always open.

How does payment work?

Cash on arrival, card on arrival, or online (Stripe link sent after the cleaner finishes). Corporate clients can opt for monthly invoicing.

Do you offer recurring cleaning?

Yes — weekly: 20% off, biweekly: 10% off. Same vendor each time. Annual contracts save up to 25%.
