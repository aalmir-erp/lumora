The honest risks

What could go wrong, and how we mitigate.

Every investor deck has a risks slide. Most are boilerplate. Below are the risks that genuinely keep me up at night — with the actual mitigation we run today.

⚔️

1. Incumbents copy the tech

Justlife has $30M to spend. They could decide to build agentic AI tomorrow. Our 18-month head start collapses if they hire fast.

MitigationData moat compounds — every RLAIF chat makes our prompts harder to copy. NFC + Wear OS are hardware moats that take quarters to ship. We close Series A before they finish feasibility studies.
💸

2. Anthropic / OpenAI pricing changes

If Claude pricing doubles, gross margins compress. We're API-dependent.

Mitigation8-provider router already cascades through DeepSeek V3 (~1/10th Claude cost). Q3 fine-tune further insulates. We are never single-provider locked.
🚨

3. AI mis-quote → Twitter incident

If Claude quotes a wrong price by AED 200, customer first call is Twitter. Brand damage compounds.

MitigationRLAIF auto-flags low-scoring replies. AI-critic scoring is independent of customer reporting. Every high-stakes action (booking, payment) has a human-loop gate.
🚐

4. Crew supply during Airbnb surge

500 hosts syncing simultaneously needs 50+ crews/day in right neighbourhoods. Supply is the binding constraint.

MitigationRecovery + reverse-auction dispatch already handles supply-demand matching. Vendor-onboarding agent automates new crew acquisition. We throttle iCal sync to match supply.
📦

5. NFC hardware fulfillment

Shipping 10,000 NFC tags requires inventory + fulfillment we haven't fully built yet.

MitigationNFC tags AED 8-15/unit at scale (3 verified UAE suppliers). Dubai-warehouse ship only. Known-cost operations, not a tech problem.
👤

6. Key-person risk

If the founder is unavailable, AI orchestration layer needs a senior eng who can maintain it.

MitigationFirst post-funding hire is Staff Engineer for AI infra. Docs + runbooks already exist. Codebase is vanilla FastAPI — no exotic patterns.
