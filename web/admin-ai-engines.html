2. Google Gemini — Extensions + SGE

Reach: Google.com SGE billions of queries · Gemini app users
⏳ Apply

Two channels to register

(a) Gemini Extensions — like ChatGPT's GPT Store, gets you mentioned when users ask Gemini in-chat.

(b) Google Search Generative Experience (SGE) — when Gemini-powered AI answers appear at the top of regular Google searches, your structured data gets pulled in.

Steps for (a) Gemini Extensions:

  1. Go to developers.google.com/gemini/docs/extensions
  2. Click "Submit a Gemini Extension" (early-access form as of v1.24.143; opens up Q2 2026)
  3. Provide: OpenAPI spec URL (same one as ChatGPT — servia.ae/openapi-public.json), brand assets, sample queries, content policy
  4. Review takes 2-4 weeks. Once approved, users invoke via @servia in Gemini app or web

Steps for (b) Google SGE / AI Overviews:

  1. Make sure structured data (Schema.org Service, LocalBusiness, FAQPage) is on every service page — already done in our rich variant LPs (v1.24.133)
  2. Submit sitemap.xml in Google Search Console: search.google.com/search-console → Sitemaps → Add new sitemap → https://servia.ae/sitemap.xml
  3. Enable "AI features for your site" toggle in Google Search Console settings (rolling out 2025-2026)
  4. Add llms.txt at root (we already serve https://servia.ae/llms.txt) — tells Gemini what content to use for AI Overviews
Benefit: When someone Googles "AC cleaning Dubai", Gemini's AI Overview can summarize Servia at the very top — above ALL competitor PPC ads.
