Common questions

How is my discount applied?

Your tier % is automatically deducted at checkout on every Servia booking. No coupon to copy. Discount stacks once per booking.

Can I stack rewards (refer + review + post)?

Yes. Each genuine action earns a tier boost or referral count. Higher tier = bigger % off every booking.

Is there a maximum tier?

Platinum is the highest, capped at 20% off. We may launch surprise pop-up tiers for power-ambassadors with bigger perks.

What counts as a 'genuine' review?

At least 1 sentence in your own words + a star rating. We don't dictate content — only honest reviews qualify.

Can businesses / corporates participate?

Yes. Corporate referrals (e.g. office-cleaning contracts) get bigger % discount on volume — submit through your account and we'll set up a custom tier.

What if I haven't booked yet but want to share?

You can still share from this page. Once your friend books, your discount activates retroactively on your first booking.
